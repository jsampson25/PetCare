# MVP Scope

## Goal

Allow a single-location boarding, daycare, and grooming business to configure its operation, accept online bookings, collect deposits, and manage the core customer and pet journey through checkout.

## P0 launch capabilities

### Business setup

- Business and location profiles
- Hours, closures, holidays, and drop-off/pickup windows
- Configurable boarding, daycare, grooming, and add-on services
- Resources and capacity
- Pricing, tax, fee, deposit, cancellation, and waitlist rules
- Vaccine and document requirements
- Staff invitations and initial roles

### Customers and pets

- Registration, sign-in, and password recovery
- Household and contact information
- Multiple pets per customer
- Pet identity, care, feeding, medical, medication, and behavior information
- Vaccination records and document uploads
- Waivers and acknowledgements

### Booking

- Availability search
- Boarding, daycare, and grooming bookings
- Multiple pets where permitted
- Add-ons
- Price calculation and immutable quote snapshot
- Required deposit collection
- Confirmation and cancellation
- Waitlist entry and staff promotion

### Operations

- Today's arrivals and departures
- Universal check-in and check-out
- Basic housing or resource assignment
- Operational alerts and care instructions
- Boarding, daycare, and grooming work views
- Feeding and medication task recording
- Staff notes and an auditable timeline

### Financial and communications

- Stripe payment collection
- Deposits, balances, invoices, refunds, and receipts
- Booking confirmations and reminders by email
- Optional SMS reminders
- Basic revenue, booking, and occupancy reporting

### Customer-facing experience

- Light-themed responsive website and customer portal
- Customer booking flow
- Upcoming and past bookings
- Pet and document management
- Invoice and payment history

## Explicitly deferred

- Native mobile applications
- Payroll and full workforce management
- Full retail POS and purchasing
- Advanced marketing automation and loyalty gamification
- Franchise tooling
- Predictive AI and autonomous agents
- Veterinary workflows
- Pet sitting and dog walking execution

## MVP guardrail

A deferred capability may shape an interface or data boundary, but it must not become a dependency for launching the core booking and operations journey.
