# Product Vision

## Vision

Build the most modern all-in-one operating platform for pet-care businesses, enabling boarding, daycare, and grooming facilities to manage their entire customer and operational lifecycle in one seamless product.

## Mission

Help pet-care businesses spend less time managing disconnected software and more time delivering safe, excellent care.

## Primary market

Independent and multi-location pet-care businesses offering one or more of:

- Dog boarding
- Dog daycare
- Dog grooming

The initial product must work particularly well for businesses that offer all three.

## Future expansion

- Pet sitting
- Dog walking
- Training
- Mobile grooming
- Retail and expanded inventory
- Franchise and enterprise administration

These extensions should influence flexibility but must not inflate the MVP.

## Core product experiences

1. **Public experience** — the business website and online booking journey.
2. **Customer experience** — accounts, pets, documents, bookings, payments, messages, and report cards.
3. **Staff experience** — arrivals, departures, daily care, grooming work, tasks, alerts, and communications.
4. **Business experience** — configuration, schedules, customers, financials, employees, reporting, and website management.
5. **Platform administration** — subscriptions, tenants, support, audit, feature rollout, and platform health.

## Differentiation

- Consistent, modern design across the website, booking flow, customer portal, and staff software
- Workflow-centered operations rather than a collection of disconnected modules
- One configurable booking model supporting boarding, daycare, and grooming
- Business-controlled pricing, deposits, waitlists, capacity, eligibility, and policies
- Strong operational safety controls and an auditable pet-care timeline
- An included customer-facing website that does not feel bolted onto the management system
- Actionable business insight, with optional AI assistance added only where it reduces work

## North-star outcome

A new business can configure its essential services and accept a valid paid booking quickly, while existing staff can safely manage the pet from arrival through departure without using another operational system.
