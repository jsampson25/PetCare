# Roventra Marketing Site Information Architecture

## Positioning

Roventra is the connected website and operating platform for modern pet-care businesses. The defining difference is that a business's public website, booking journey, customer account, and daily operating workspace share one platform and one brand system.

## Capability strategy

Roventra must meet market expectations for service-aware booking, scheduling, pricing, capacity, memberships, packages, payments, communication, AI assistance, and multi-location control. It differentiates through additional depth in these areas:

1. Full customizable business websites with themes, visual sections, custom pages, domains, publishing, and search controls.
2. Brand continuity across public pages, booking, registration, payments, messaging, report cards, and the customer portal.
3. Daily animal-care operations covering feeding, medication, playgroups, enrichment, wellness, belongings, incidents, and report cards.
4. Facility operations covering kennels, cleaning, sanitation, inspections, maintenance, assets, environments, and turnover.
5. Customer growth covering journeys, campaigns, loyalty, referrals, reviews, memberships, packages, and customer analytics.
6. Commerce coverage spanning catalog, point of sale, inventory, purchasing, vendors, transfers, returns, gift cards, and promotions.
7. Enterprise controls spanning tenants, locations, roles, policies, audit, configuration, feature flags, security, integrations, and data governance.

## Publishing rules

- Clearly distinguish available, beta, planned, and future capabilities.
- Never claim a certification, integration, or production feature without evidence.
- Use original language, diagrams, and interface visuals.
- Lead with buyer outcomes, then show operational depth.
- Give every major service its own landing page and realistic workflow examples.
- Keep pricing, product eligibility, and checkout terms consistent with platform entitlements.
