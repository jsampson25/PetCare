# Product Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Business | The subscribing pet-care organization; the primary tenant boundary. |
| Location | A physical facility operated by a business. |
| Customer | A person with an account or relationship with a business. |
| Household | A group of customers who may share pets, billing, or portal access. |
| Pet | The animal receiving services. The MVP focuses on dogs. |
| Service | A configurable offering such as overnight boarding, full-day daycare, or full grooming. |
| Add-on | An optional service added to a booking, such as a nail trim or private playtime. |
| Booking | The customer transaction that groups pets, services, dates, price, policies, and payments. |
| Booking item | A specific service for a pet within a booking. |
| Stay | The operational period during which a checked-in pet is in the business's care. |
| Resource | Something whose availability may constrain a service, such as a kennel, suite, groomer, table, or play area. |
| Capacity | The configurable quantity available for a service, resource, or time period. |
| Waitlist entry | A customer's request to be considered when unavailable capacity opens. |
| Deposit | Money required before a booking is confirmed, governed by a snapshotted policy. |
| Eligibility | Whether a customer or pet meets the requirements for a service. |
| Policy snapshot | The immutable version of a rule or agreement accepted for a booking. |
| Platform admin | An authorized operator of the SaaS platform, distinct from a business owner. |
